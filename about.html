Welcome

From early childhood I was deeply interested in technology, mainly computer science and video games. I was constantly learning something interesting and building my curiosity when exploring early operating systems and never really thought deeply about whether I can really learn this stuff, proggraming.
I just surfed the internet, watched for technological news, fixed bugs or errors and modded games in deep absorption. After graduating ICT technical school and learning there the basics of C++ programming I was lost. I didn't really think if I could make it in this vast field. It just remained a hobby so I became a police officer because I like contacts with people, helping them and building physical fitness.
One time something changed. From that moment my grit is ever evolving. I decided to go with my interests and start from scratch, learning Web Development and never looked back. When I realised how much there is to learn and that I can get better at it if I keep learning I decided to quit my duty and learn proggraming full time. I got exited.
